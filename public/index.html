About Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon known for its adorable appearance and unique features. Not only does he look cute, but he also has a tough little dinosaur vibe that sets him apart from other Pokémon.

He is my favorite Pokémon because of his charming looks and strength. Bulbasaur's design balances both adorable and fierce, making him a fantastic companion in the Pokémon world!
