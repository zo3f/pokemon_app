About Bulbasaur

Bulbasaur is a dual-type Grass/Poison Pokémon known for its adorable appearance and unique features.

Behind the cute face is a tough little dinosaur vibe. That mix of charm and strength is exactly why Bulbasaur is my favorite starter.

This app is a small playground to learn JavaScript, Node, and modern front-end techniques using Bulbasaur as the mascot.

Play Game Boy Advance Games

Select a local .gba ROM file and click Play ROM. The game runs completely in your browser using EmulatorJS.

Your game screen will appear here after you load a ROM.

ROM Usage & Tips

  • Use your own legally obtained backups of games you own.
  • ROMs never leave your device; they are loaded directly from your browser.
  • For now, ROMs are not stored server-side – each refresh means you reselect the file.
